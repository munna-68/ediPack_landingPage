About EdiPack

We design practical compostable packaging for modern food service. Our focus is simple: make sustainable choices easier to adopt at scale.

Our Mission View Products

Our Mission

EdiPack exists to reduce packaging waste in everyday food service. We partner with restaurants, cafes, and hospitality teams to replace single-use plastics with compostable alternatives that are strong, reliable, and easy for staff to use.

What We Value

Material Integrity

Every product is designed for dependable food-service performance while supporting compostable end-of-life outcomes.

Operational Practicality

We prioritize solutions that fit real kitchen and takeaway workflows, not sustainability ideas that are hard to run.

Long-Term Partnership

From trial packs to scale-up phases, our team supports clients with clear guidance and responsive supply planning.
